Our Canadian Road Trip!

Are we there yet?


Kingston - the best and worse of times.

Adventures in "The Nice Chicago" we visit Toronto. Canadians are lovely people - sweet, helpful, considerate.
We also visited the AGO museum and saw this wonderful Frank Gehry design.

Feeling like we have the Smugglers Blues, crossing border at 1am. We have no contraband but a reasonable amount of Coca-Cola we do have in the trunk, officer. Everything went fine and we had re-entry.

☆ Highlights in Kingston and before Toronto ☆

  • This Coca-Cola is wonderful. It's both sweet and smooth.
  • The kids get the master bedroom in Kingston, complete with a jacuzzi!
  • The most lovely beach and dock area. See the baby ducks and the birds in a wonderful quaint setting! ⚓ ⚓
  • A super lunch complete with Lobster Rolls. Yes, please I would love to sit outside and enjoy the historical building.

☆ Low Spots in Kingston and before Toronto ☆

  • The bugs descend at night!!
  • We get the last room at the hotel and its not to our taste.
  • Stop and I mean stop trying to add Gravy to our food.
  • Can Daddy tell time?

☆ Highlights in Toronto and beyond ☆

  • Our hotel in Toronto is understated but very nice.
  • One of my best breakfasts ever!! I learn about a great coffee company whose product they use.
  • AGO museum is terrific - a Warhol, Richter, Canadian masterworks, and a terrific high-low experience - arriving
    by hotel limo to the Wednesday night free admission time.

☆ Low Spots in Toronto and beyond ☆

  • I think I had one too many Tim's I can't sleep
  • Maybe five-too-many stops at the highway rest stop.
  • Daddy, are you sure we should be trying to cross the border at an hour after midnight?
